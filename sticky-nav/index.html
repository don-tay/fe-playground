About

Donatello's Pizzeria was founded in 1997 by Donatello de Firenze. Growing up as a dough boy in a pizzeria in his hometown in Naples, Donatello picked up the ropes of creating pizza. Under the mentorship of his father, Ezio Auditore de Firenze, Donatello has established himself to be one of the most prominent pizzamaker in all of Italy. He attributes his skills to decades of trial and error, and his ability to fuse new food trends into the traditional Neapolitan Pizza without transgressing cultural appropriation. With 23 outlets in 15 countries, Donatello Pizzeria is bringing your Italian favourites to your doorstep!
